# Flourish 2

This visualization shows the distribution of building permit types issued by the City of Seattle. Each bar represents the number of permits within a specific class, allowing for comparison across categories such as single-family/duplex, commercial, and multifamily developments. The bar chart makes it easy to identify dominant permit types while also highlighting less frequent categories and any unlabeled entries in the dataset.

Source: [Seattle Open Data](https://data.seattle.gov/Built-Environment/Building-Permits/76t5-zqzr/about_data)
